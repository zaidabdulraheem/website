My Case Studies

Detailed Work Analysis

AI-Driven Insights Dashboard

Problem

The client, a medium-sized e-commerce business, struggled with understanding their vast array of customer data. They were unable to derive actionable insights due to the sheer volume and complexity of their data.

Solution

Developed a Vue.js based interactive dashboard that not only visualized their e-commerce metrics but also integrated a Machine Learning model. This model analyzed customer behavior, predicting potential high-value customers and recommending marketing strategies tailored to individual customer segments.

Results

The AI-driven dashboard led to a 30% increase in marketing efficiency, a 15% increase in customer retention, and a 25% boost in sales over a 6-month period.

3D Product Visualization Tool

Problem

An online furniture store faced high return rates because customers felt the 2D images didn't represent the actual product accurately.

Solution

Using Three.js, we developed an interactive 3D visualization tool that allowed customers to view furniture products from all angles, zoom in to see texture details, and even place the furniture in a virtual room setup.

Results

The immersive 3D product experience led to a 50% reduction in return rates and a 20% increase in customer satisfaction scores.

Personalized Content Recommender

Problem

A popular online content platform was experiencing a decline in user engagement. They realized users often felt overwhelmed with the vast amount of content and didn't get relevant recommendations.

Solution

Implemented a Machine Learning recommendation system that analyzed user behavior, content preferences, and browsing patterns. The system provided personalized content suggestions, ensuring users always found relevant and engaging material.

Results

Post-integration, the platform saw a 40% increase in user engagement, with users spending, on average, 20 minutes more on the platform daily.
